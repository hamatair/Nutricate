Grilled Turkey Breast with Steamed Asparagus and Brown Rice
Lunch
A lean and balanced meal that's perfect for a post-workout lunch or a healthy midday option. This meal provides a great balance of protein, fiber, and healthy fats while being low in calories. The grilled turkey breast offers a rich source of lean protein, while the steamed asparagus and brown rice provide essential vitamins and minerals.
Tools & Equipment
  • 1Grill pan or outdoor grill
  • 2Medium pot for steaming
  • 3Saucepan for cooking brown rice
  • 4Tongs for turning turkey
  • 5Cutting board
  • 6Knife
Directions
  • 1
    Prepare the Turkey
    Season the turkey breast with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Set aside.
  • 2
    Cook the Brown Rice
    In a saucepan, cook 1/2 cup of brown rice in 1 cup of water. Let it simmer for 15 minutes or until fully cooked.
  • 3
    Grill the Turkey
    Preheat the grill pan over medium heat. Grill the turkey breast for 6–7 minutes on each side until fully cooked (internal temp: 165°F).
  • 4
    Steam the Asparagus
    In a medium pot, steam the asparagus for 5 minutes or until tender.
  • 5
    Serve and Garnish
    Arrange the turkey breast, brown rice, and asparagus on a plate. Garnish with a lemon wedge.
Notes
  • For added flavor, marinate the turkey in lemon juice and garlic for 30 minutes before grilling.
  • You can replace brown rice with quinoa or couscous for variety.
